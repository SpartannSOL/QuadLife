When the Musa Tribe arrived from Uribin X, Led by the great warrior NakNak, they found a planet full of disparaging ignorance plaguing it’s inhabitants. NakNak began interacting with the planets beings and simultaneously delivered the wisdom acquired from the universe to those with pure energy.

NakNak viewed earth and all living beings as a creation deriving of a higher power mightier than him in which all beings can experience universal knowledge from. When NakNak started emitting these frequencies he noticed his ability began connecting to the natural frequencies embedded in every beings experience of living.

His immense vibrational frequency emitting became so powerful, that when in unison with the Musa Tribe of Quadrillionaire Apes, Great Miracles of time bending phenomena occurred.

Through these encounters the humanoids developed a greater understanding of the universe and the power of beings.
